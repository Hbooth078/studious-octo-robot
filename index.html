Game descriptions

Fortnite is a free-to-play, cross-platform title from Epic Games that fuses fast-paced shooting with real-time building. Players skydive onto a vibrant island, scavenge weapons and resources, and erect walls, ramps, or towers to gain a tactical edge. The goal in its flagship Battle Royale mode is simple: be the last person or squad standing as an ever-shrinking storm closes in. Hogwarts Legacy an open-world action RPG set in the 1890s wizarding world where you play a customizable fifth-year student, master spells, explore Hogwarts and its surroundings, and shape the story through your choices. Stardew Valley a farming and life simulation in which you restore a neglected farm, tend crops and animals, mine and fish for resources, and build relationships with the townspeople to revive the community. Animal Crossing: New Horizons a relaxed island-life simulator where you gather materials, craft furniture and tools, customize terrain and decor, befriend anthropomorphic villagers, and enjoy real-time seasonal and daily events.
